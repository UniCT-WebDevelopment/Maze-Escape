Maze Escape
Welcome to Maze Escape!
This game is a multiplayer survival horror in which one player controls a survivor and another player controls the maze's Entity.
The rules are as follows
---------------
Survivor:
You'll have to find and press three pressure plates to activate the black portal, escape the maze, and access the secret treasure room.
Be careful: The Entity lurks within the maze. If it hits you three times, it's game over.
Controls:
W to walk forward;
A to turn left;
S to walk backward;
D to turn right;
CTRL to crouch.
---------------
Entity:
You're the guardian of the maze.
Your objective is to kill every intruder you find to protect the maze's secret treasure.
You'll only need three hits to kill someone, but be fast or the treasure could be stolen.
To hit the survivor, all you have to do is touch him.
Controls:
W to walk forward;
A to turn left;
S to walk backward;
D to turn right;
SPACE to punch.
---------------
After you set a username, you'll be able to either play with a friend by sending a game request, or with a casual player by starting an online matchmaking session.
Every game will be unique, thanks to the random generation of the maze and the random placement of objects within it.
For example, pressure plates can spawn anywhere in the maze, but the exit will only appear on the borders after the survivor presses three pressure plates.
---------------
I hope you'll have fun playing my game.
Enjoy!
